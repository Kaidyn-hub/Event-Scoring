About Us

Welcome to our platform dedicated to enhancing the experience of sports enthusiasts, event organizers, and participants through improved scoreboard accessibility and usability.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a web-based platform that makes scoreboards more accessible and user-friendly for everyone. We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to easily access and understand the scores and statistics of their favorite events.

What We Do

This website is designed as part of our research titled "Improving Scoreboard Accessibility and Usability Through a Web-Based Platform." Our goal is to address the challenges faced by users when accessing scoreboard information, particularly in terms of clarity, ease of navigation, and overall user experience.

We aim to provide a solution that not only displays scores but also enhances the way users interact with this information. By leveraging modern web technologies, we strive to create an intuitive interface that caters to a diverse audience, including those with disabilities.

Why It Matters

Scoreboards are essential for keeping track of scores in various sports and events. However, traditional scoreboard systems can often be difficult to read and navigate, especially for individuals with visual impairments or those unfamiliar with the layout. Our platform seeks to bridge this gap by offering:

  • Clear and accessible score displays
  • User-friendly navigation
  • Responsive design for mobile and desktop users
  • Features that cater to users with disabilities

Get Involved

We invite you to explore our platform and provide feedback. Your insights are invaluable as we work towards refining our approach and ensuring that our solution meets the needs of all users. Together, we can make scoreboard information accessible to everyone!
